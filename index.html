Phase 01 Foundation Weeks 1–4
Before any automation or ad spend, the plumbing has to be right. Most DTC brands skip this and wonder why their results are inconsistent. Phase 1 is about installing the infrastructure that every subsequent phase depends on.
1.1 Tracking & Data Infrastructure
Meta Conversions API (CAPI) — Most Important Single Task
Enable Pixel + CAPI dual tracking integration
Verify event deduplication — Ensure purchases aren't double-counted when both Pixel and CAPI fire
Enable Enhanced Event Match Quality (EMQ) — Send hashed email, phone, and name with every purchase event
Set up Value-Based Optimization — Tell Meta which orders are worth more — kit buyers vs. single accessory buyers
Validate events in Meta Events Manager — Confirm ViewContent, AddToCart, InitiateCheckout, and Purchase all fire correctly
Why This Matters First
iOS privacy changes have blocked the browser pixel for 40–60% of visitors. Without CAPI, Meta is flying blind — and charging full price while doing it. Brands that implement CAPI see 20–35% more reported conversions and lower CPAs within 2–3 weeks because the algorithm has better data to learn from.
Google Analytics 4 + Enhanced Conversions
Verify GA4 purchase events fire on every Shopify order — Use GA4 debugger to confirm
Enable Google Ads Enhanced Conversions — Sends hashed customer email to Google for better attribution
Link Google Ads to GA4 and Google Merchant Center — Required for Performance Max
Set up Google Merchant Center product feed — Clean titles, accurate GTINs, correct pricing — missing GTINs cut Shopping impressions up to 40%
Configure conversion goals — Purchase (primary), Add to Cart (secondary), Initiate Checkout (secondary)

1.5 Offer Architecture & Discount Rules

Without defined rules, discount chaos sets in fast. Multiple incentives floating independently — welcome discount, win-back offer, referral credit, comment reply code — create gaps customers will exploit and undercut B&F's premium positioning. These rules must be established before any flow goes live.

Channel Max Discount Preferred Format Notes
Welcome Series 10–15% % off or free shipping Email 2 of 3 only
Abandoned Cart 10% max Email 3 only (escalation) No discount on emails 1–2
Win-Back Flow 15% max % off or $ credit Email 3 only
Referral Program $15 store credit $ store credit (referrer reward) Paid after referee ships
Ad Comment Reply 10% max % off first purchase only Purchase-question replies only
Hard Rules (Non-Negotiable)
1
One discount per customer per order — Shopify codes set to single-use only. A customer who signs up via the welcome flow cannot stack a cart abandonment code on the same order.
2
No discount on bundles or kits — Hat + Towel bundles are excluded from all percentage-off codes. Discounting bundles collapses margin with no offsetting AOV benefit.
3
Discount hierarchy — If a customer is eligible for multiple triggers simultaneously, apply only the highest-priority one: Welcome > Cart Recovery > Win-Back. Never layer them.
4
Absolute ceiling is 15% — No channel, flow, or campaign should ever offer more than 15% off a single product. This protects the brand's premium price positioning and ensures margin at scale.
5
Default to free shipping over % off — When in doubt, free shipping delivers equivalent perceived value to customers at lower margin impact.

2.1 Meta Ads Architecture

The playbook in 2025–26 is counterintuitive: do less manual work, feed the algorithm better inputs, and let Advantage+ do what it's actually become good at.

Campaign A
Advantage+ Shopping Campaign (ASC)
Prospecting + retargeting in one. Go broad. No audience restrictions.
Load 8–12 creatives across Reels, statics, and carousels.
This is your main growth engine.
Campaign B
Dynamic Product Ads (Catalog)
Automated retargeting using your Shopify product catalog.
Shows site visitors the exact product they viewed.
Zero ongoing management after setup.
Campaign C
Engagement Retargeting
Small budget. Targets people who watched 50%+ of your Reels or engaged with B&F Instagram in the last 30 days.
Social proof ads only — reviews, creator content.
Minimum Budget to Enter Meta Learning Phase
Meta's ASC needs approximately $500/week ($70/day) to exit the learning phase within 7–10 days. Below this, the algorithm doesn't get enough conversion events to optimize. Start there and scale only after the campaign stabilizes at a consistent ROAS.
Creative System — The #1 Lever in 2025
Produce 5 new creatives every 2 weeks minimum — Mix: 2 Reels (15–30s), 2 statics, 1 carousel. Creative accounts for 56% of campaign outcomes.
Test across 5 angles simultaneously — Performance, Lifestyle (Nashville aesthetic), Premium/Craftsmanship, Gift angle, Social proof/Reviews
Hook in the first 2 seconds — Bold text overlay calling out a benefit before anyone can scroll past.
No multiple CTAs per ad — One ad, one job. 'Shop the Origin' not 'Shop, Follow us, and Tag a Friend'
Do not touch audiences or bids — Your only ongoing job is refreshing creative every 2–3 weeks before fatigue sets in
2.2 Google Ads Architecture
Campaign 1
Brand Search
Targets 'Brawn and Fox,' 'Brawn Fox hat,' and all branded variants.
Small budget (~$5–10/day). Exact match.
Protects brand traffic from being cannibalized by PMax.
Campaign 2
Category Search
Targets 'premium golf hat,' 'golf snapback,' 'magnetic golf towel,' 'performance golf headwear.'
Phrase and broad match with negative keywords.
High-intent buyers actively shopping the category.
Campaign 3
Performance Max
Feeds off your Merchant Center product catalog.
Reaches across Search, YouTube, Gmail, Display, and Discover from one campaign.
Run at least 3 asset groups: Gift Angle, Performance Angle, Lifestyle/Nashville Angle.
PMax Asset Group Setup — Each Group Needs:
5 headlines (short, punchy, keyword-rich)
5 long headlines (benefit-led, 90 characters)
5 descriptions (different angles and CTAs)
At minimum: 20 product/lifestyle images in 1:1 and 4:3
At minimum: 1 video under 30 seconds in both 1:1 and 16:9
Audience signal: upload your Klaviyo customer list as a seed audience
Critical: Exclude Brand Keywords from PMax
Without explicit brand exclusions, Performance Max will spend a significant portion of your budget on branded searches — people who were already going to buy from you. Apply brand keyword exclusions at the campaign level (now possible after Google's January 2025 update).

2.4 n8n Automation Batch 1 — Core Workflows

These five automations are the highest ROI, lowest complexity workflows to deploy first. Each has a proven n8n template or straightforward build path.

01
Daily Revenue & Performance Briefing
Runs every morning at 7am via Schedule trigger
Pulls Shopify orders from prior day: revenue, order count, AOV, top SKU
Fetches Meta Ads spend and ROAS from Graph API
OpenAI generates 3-sentence plain-English summary with one action item
Posts complete briefing to Chuck's Slack DM
Time to build: 3–4 hrs · Replaces 30min daily manual check
02
Inventory-Synced Ad Pausing (3-Tier)
Shopify inventory webhook triggers on every stock change
20+ units: All campaigns run normally
5–20 units: Catalog feed updated with 'Limited Stock' urgency text overlay
0–5 units: All Meta campaigns pause automatically
When stock replenished >20: Campaigns reactivate with previous budgets
Time to build: 4–5 hrs · Impact: $200–800/mo in prevented wasted spend
03
Abandoned Cart Recovery (Email + SMS)
Shopify abandoned cart webhook → check customer history and segment
1 hour: Personalized email via Klaviyo with dynamic discount based on segment
3 hours: SMS via Postscript if no purchase
24 hours: Final SMS nudge (WhatsApp added in Phase 3 once volume justifies it)
VIP carts that don't convert: hash email + upload to Meta Custom Audience
Expected lift: 15–25% above email-only recovery · Template: n8n #11805
04
Review Intelligence Aggregator
Okendo webhook fires when new review submitted
OpenAI classifies: sentiment and topic (fit, quality, style, delivery, gifting)
Negative reviews: instant Slack alert to Chuck with suggested response draft
Positive 5-star reviews: trigger Klaviyo event to invite customer to ambassador program
Weekly digest: top themes and actionable insights
Time to build: 3–4 hrs · Never miss a negative review; surface UGC opportunities
05
DIY Referral Engine
Trigger: customer places 2nd order
Generate unique Shopify discount code (e.g., CHUCK10 format)
Send branded email: 'You're a B&F insider — share your code'
When code used: log referrer + referee in Google Sheets
After referred order ships: email referrer a $15 store credit
Replaces $50–200/mo referral app subscription · Template: n8n #10161

3.1 n8n Automation Batch 2 — Intelligence Layer
06
Creative Fatigue Detection + Alert System
Runs daily — pulls all active Meta ad metrics via Graph API
Alert triggers when: frequency > 2.5 OR CTR drops > 30% WoW OR CPP increases > 40% WoW
Slack alert: 'Creative [X] showing fatigue. Frequency 2.8. Recommend replacing within 7 days.'
Weekly creative performance digest: ranking of all active creatives by ROAS
Replaces reactive firefighting with 7–14 day advance warning
07
Meta Winning Creative Auto-Scaler
When a creative hits ROAS > 3x for 3 consecutive days with meaningful spend
Slack alert with creative details + one-click approval button to Chuck
If approved: Graph API duplicates the ad set to 2 new audiences
New ad sets launch at 50% of original budget to avoid disrupting learning phase
Eliminates the delay between 'we have a winner' and 'we're scaling it'
08
Golf Event Seasonal Budget Bursts
Google Sheets calendar stores all major golf events (Masters, US Open, PGA Championship, Ryder Cup...)
7 days before each event: Slack message with suggested budget increase
If Chuck approves: Google Ads and Meta APIs apply budget increase automatically
Budget reverts automatically on the event's end date
Also triggers Google Ads Seasonality Adjustment for aggressive PMax bidding
09
Ad Comment Intelligence Bot
Monitors all Meta ad comments every 30 minutes via Graph API
OpenAI classifies: Enthusiastic / Purchase Question / Objection / Negative
Purchase Question: automatic reply with product link + 10% first-purchase code within minutes
Negative/complaint: immediate Slack escalation — never auto-responded
25% of purchases happen after a brand responds. Most brands respond in days, not minutes.
10
Predictive Win-Back Intelligence
Runs weekly — pulls all customers whose last purchase was 60–120 days ago
OpenAI generates churn risk score and recommended intervention
High-risk, high-value (2+ orders, AOV > $80): personal text from Chuck via Postscript
Lower-risk: route to standard Klaviyo win-back sequence
Expected 20–30% higher win-back rate for VIP segment vs. generic flow
11
Golf Influencer / Creator Discovery Pipeline
Runs weekly — Apify scrapes Instagram under golf hashtags in last 7 days
Filters to micro-influencer sweet spot: 2,000–50,000 followers
OpenAI scores each profile for B&F brand fit
Top 10 logged to Google Sheet with AI-generated outreach message
Replaces 4–6 hrs/week of manual Instagram scrolling. One creator = $500–2,000 attributed sales.
12
SEO-Optimized Product Description Writer
Trigger: New product created or published in Shopify
Pulls product title, variant details, and raw notes
OpenAI generates 150–200 word SEO-optimized description in B&F brand voice
Also generates: meta description (under 160 chars), 5 title tag variants
Auto-pushes completed copy back to Shopify via API

3.2 Advanced Ads — Measurement & Optimization
Customer Match Sync
Every Shopify purchase → upload hashed email to Meta Custom Audience (excludes them from prospecting)
Same hashed email → Google Ads Customer Match (+30–50% bid boost when past customers search golf terms)
Both platforms always have a fresh, accurate list. No manual CSV exports.
Monthly Geo Holdout Test
Once a month, create Meta campaign targeting all US states except a small control group
Control group sees zero B&F ads for 14 days
n8n pulls Shopify sales by state, compares ad-exposed vs. holdout
Output: your real ROAS — not the one Meta reports
Creative Angle Testing
Define 5 permanent creative angles: Performance, Lifestyle, Premium, Gift, Social Proof
Every 2 weeks: one new creative per angle (5 minimum)
n8n weekly digest tracks which angles are winning
Winning frameworks get reused with new product variants and seasonal hooks
